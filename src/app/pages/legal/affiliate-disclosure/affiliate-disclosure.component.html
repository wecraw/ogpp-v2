How affiliate links work

Some of the links to products and retailers on OGPP are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission from the retailer — at no extra cost to you. The price you pay is the same whether or not you use our link.

How it affects our recommendations

It doesn't. OGPP sizes and filters gear based on your appliances and location — the math is the same regardless of which products carry an affiliate relationship. We don't reorder, inflate, or bias recommendations to favor higher commissions.

Confirm details before you buy

Prices, specifications, and availability shown here are curated by hand and can fall out of date. Always confirm the current details on the retailer's own page before purchasing. We are not the seller and are not responsible for your transaction with the retailer.
