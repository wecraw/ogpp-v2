Off Grid Part Picker ("OGPP", "we", "us") is a free tool that helps you size and choose an off-grid solar power system. We built this policy to be short and honest: we keep your data on your own device and collect as little as possible. This page explains what that means in practice.

The short version

  • There is no account and no sign-up. We don't ask for your name or email.
  • Your builds are saved in your browser's local storage — on your device, not on a server.
  • We send your ZIP code to a third-party solar-irradiance service to estimate the sunlight in your area. We don't store it ourselves.
  • We never sell or share your personal information.

Information we process

Builds and preferences

The appliances you select, your chosen seasons, ZIP code, gear selections, and theme preference are saved to your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device. It is not transmitted to us, and clearing your browser data removes it permanently.

Location (ZIP code)

To estimate how much sunlight your location receives, we send the ZIP code you enter to the U.S. National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) solar-resource API. We use the result to size your system and we do not retain the ZIP code on any server of ours. Their handling of that request is governed by NREL's own privacy practices.

Affiliate and outbound links

When you choose to buy gear, we link you out to the manufacturer or retailer. Some of these are affiliate links, which may append tracking parameters so the retailer can credit a referral. Once you leave our site, the destination retailer's privacy policy applies. See our Affiliate Disclosure for details.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell, rent, or trade your information.
  • We don't require you to create an account.
  • We don't run third-party advertising trackers on your builds.

Your choices

Because your data lives in your browser, you are in control of it. You can delete individual builds from the Builds page, or clear all OGPP data by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

Children's privacy

OGPP is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. When we do, we'll revise the "last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected here.
